## Mission

Demokritos Scientific Journal is an open scientific publishing initiative designed to make high-quality research in physics and mathematics accessible to the global scientific community.

The journal promotes:

- open scientific discovery
- constructive peer review
- mentorship for emerging researchers
- global accessibility to scientific knowledge

Our goal is not simply to publish papers but to build a **collaborative research ecosystem**.

---

## Why This Journal Exists

Traditional academic publishing often creates barriers:

- high publication fees
- anonymous peer review that rejects rather than helps
- limited accessibility to research results

Demokritos Scientific Journal was created to address these problems.

Our philosophy is simple:

**Good research should be improved, not rejected prematurely.**

We focus on helping authors develop their work to publishable quality whenever possible.

---

## Constructive Peer Review Model

Peer review is essential to scientific publishing, but many journals use it primarily as a rejection mechanism.

Our model is different.

Key principles:

- reviewers are **known to authors** to encourage professional dialogue
- reviewers provide **constructive feedback** rather than simple rejection
- authors are encouraged to **revise and improve their work**

The objective is to **teach and improve research**, not simply filter it.

---

## Young Scientists Program

The journal includes a dedicated **Young Scientists Section** for high-school researchers.

Students conducting original scientific work may submit research papers.

Benefits include:

- **free publication** for student research
- mentorship-oriented peer review
- early experience with scientific publishing
- global visibility for young researchers

The goal is to **help build the next generation of scientists**.

---

## Open Research Fellowship

The **Demokritos Open Research Fellowship** invites participation from:

- independent researchers
- graduate students
- early-career scientists
- high-school researchers

Fellows collaborate on research projects, participate in peer review, and contribute to the journal’s open science community.

---

## Publication Fees

The journal follows a **flexible publication model**.

Authors may pay a small publication fee to cover infrastructure costs such as DOI registration and hosting.

However, **financial limitations should never prevent publication**.

Fee waivers are available for:

- students
- independent researchers
- authors without institutional support

Sponsors help support these waivers.

## Technology Platform

The journal platform integrates modern research tools:

- AI-assisted reviewer matching
- LaTeX-native publishing workflow
- Physics Knowledge Graph connecting papers and topics
- Equation Index of Physics across published research
- Citation network explorer

These tools transform the journal into a **research discovery platform**, not just a paper archive.

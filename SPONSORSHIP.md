## Why Support Demokritos Scientific Journal

Demokritos Scientific Journal is being developed as an open-access scientific publishing platform for **Advanced Theoretical Physics and Mathematics**.

The journal is built around five core principles:

- open scientific access
- constructive peer review
- support for emerging researchers
- technological innovation in publishing
- global scientific participation

Your support helps build not only a journal, but a **research infrastructure initiative** designed to improve how scientific knowledge is published, discovered, and taught.

---

## What Sponsor Support Helps Fund

Sponsor contributions help support:

- free publication through the **Zenodo DOI pathway**
- journal platform development and maintenance
- the **Young Scientists Section** for high-school researchers
- the **Demokritos Open Research Fellowship**
- AI-assisted reviewer tools
- the **Equation Index of Physics**
- the **Physics Knowledge Graph**
- sponsor-backed open science outreach and visibility

## Publication Model

Demokritos Scientific Journal provides two publication options:

### Journal-Issued DOI
- **$25 fee**
- DOI issued directly through the journal
- article published on the journal platform

### Free Publication with Zenodo DOI
- **$0 fee**
- DOI provided through **Zenodo**
- article published on the journal platform

This model ensures that authors always have a path to publication while keeping the platform open and accessible.

## Young Scientists Section

One of the journal’s most important missions is to support **young scientists in high school**.

This section allows student researchers to submit original scientific papers for:

- peer review
- mentoring-based revision
- free publication
- early participation in scientific research culture

We do not view peer review simply as a gatekeeping mechanism.

We work to help authors **improve and repair their work whenever possible**, rather than focusing only on rejection.

Support for this section helps build stronger future scientists.

---

## Constructive Peer Review Philosophy

Demokritos Scientific Journal uses a **constructive and transparent review model**.

Key principles:

- reviewers are **known to authors**
- review is intended to improve the work
- revisions are encouraged whenever possible
- the goal is to teach, strengthen, and develop scientific writing and argumentation

This model creates accountability, improves dialogue, and helps authors produce stronger final papers.

## Editorial Independence

Sponsor support **does not influence editorial decisions**.

All submissions are handled according to the journal’s editorial and peer-review standards.

Financial support strengthens access and infrastructure, but it does not determine acceptance, rejection, or scientific evaluation.
